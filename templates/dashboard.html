About Deepfake Detection

Our deepfake detection system uses advanced AI algorithms to analyze videos and determine if they have been manipulated. The system looks for subtle inconsistencies in facial movements, lighting, and other visual artifacts that are typically present in deepfake videos.

The technology combines computer vision and deep learning to provide accurate results in real-time, helping users identify potentially manipulated content.

Video Morphing Technology

Video morphing is a sophisticated technique that involves transforming one image or video into another through a seamless transition. While this technology has legitimate uses in film and entertainment, it can also be misused to create deceptive content.

Our detection system specifically looks for signs of morphing techniques, including:

  • Facial feature inconsistencies
  • Unnatural movement patterns
  • Lighting and texture anomalies
  • Audio-visual synchronization issues
